How Our AI-Enhanced Grading Works

At Nova Grading Co., every card goes through a dual-stage grading system designed for unmatched accuracy and consistency. Our proprietary AI Star-Scan technology performs a microscopic inspection of your card, analyzing surface defects, centering alignment, corner sharpness, and edge quality with precision far beyond the human eye. This advanced scan generates a baseline score backed by thousands of data points.

After the AI evaluation, each card is reviewed by one of our expert Master Graders who verify authenticity, assess eye appeal, and make final adjustments. The result is a balanced, transparent, and trustworthy grade you can feel confident adding to your collection.

You can expect a clean, premium slab, detailed subgrades, and full digital verification for every Nova-graded card.
