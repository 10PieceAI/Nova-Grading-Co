The Nano Standard: AI Precision Meets Human Expertise

We offer a state-of-the-art grading system that ensures consistency, objectivity, and accuracy for every single card.

  • **Centering:** High-resolution optical scanners capture millimetric measurements to determine perfect centering ratios.
  • **Surface:** Deep learning algorithms detect micro-scratches, print defects, and imperfections invisible to the naked eye.
  • **Corners & Edges:** Automated comparison against factory standard dimensions to ensure sharp, clean edges and corners.

The AI provides a baseline score (0.0 to 10.0) based on objective data points.
